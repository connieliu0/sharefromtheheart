Share from the heart event details

a chill demo share night of anything you want (art, writing, websites, songs, WIPs) as long as you made it and it's from your heart.

sign up here!

What is this?

Share your work from your heart on [DATE] at [TIME] and [LOC]! Extremely chill 3 min presentation that shows the following things:

  • what you made
  • why you made it
  • how you made it
  • where you want to go next
  • any open ended questions or thoughts

depending on the group size, can split into smaller groups around common themes for more chill sharing. at the end we'll do an open mingle / discussion!

General guidelines:

  • the focus is less on craft and more on the intention
  • be kind, sharing work is pretty scary so please exercise with care

Why do you create?

I like to believe it's because we have something we want to materialize in the world!
